Who We Are

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization of students engaged in STEM. Our chapter at Myers Park High School provides exciting opportunities for members to apply and improve their technological knowledge and skills.

At Myers Park TSA, we believe in learning by doing. Our members gain valuable experience through hands-on projects, competitions, and leadership opportunities. We focus on developing technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and teamwork.

Our club is open to all students interested in technology, engineering, and design. Whether you're a coding expert, a robotics enthusiast, or just curious about technology, there's a place for you in our club.
