Membership Information

Learn about the requirements and process for joining the Myers Park TSA Club.

Meeting Schedule

We meet every Tuesday and Thursday after school from 3:00 PM to 4:30 PM in Room 215 (Technology Lab).

Membership Dues

Annual membership dues are $40, which covers:

  • National TSA registration ($15)
  • State TSA registration ($10)
  • Chapter t-shirt ($15)

Financial assistance is available for students who need it. Please speak with our advisor.

Eligibility

Membership is open to all Myers Park High School students in grades 9-12 who have an interest in technology and innovation.

No prior experience is required! We welcome students of all skill levels.

Time Commitment

Members are expected to:

  • Attend at least 75% of regular meetings
  • Participate in at least one competition event
  • Contribute to at least one community service project

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining and participating in TSA.

What is TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization of students engaged in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics). Our chapter at Myers Park High School provides opportunities for students to develop technical skills, leadership abilities, and career readiness through competitions, projects, and community service.

Do I need to have technology experience to join?

No prior experience is required! We welcome students of all skill levels. Our club is designed to help you learn and develop skills in various technology areas. We have members who are just beginning their tech journey and others who have been coding or building robots for years.

What competitions do TSA members participate in?

TSA offers over 70 competitive events across various technology categories including robotics, coding, engineering, digital media, and more. Our chapter typically participates in regional competitions in January, state competitions in March, and qualified members may advance to the National TSA Conference in June. Visit our Competitions page for more details.

How much time commitment is required?

We meet twice a week after school (Tuesdays and Thursdays from 3:00-4:30 PM). Members are expected to attend at least 75% of meetings. Additional time may be required as competitions approach or for specific projects. We understand that students have other commitments, and we work to accommodate various schedules.

Are there financial assistance options for membership dues?

Yes, we have financial assistance available for students who need it. We believe that financial constraints should not prevent any interested student from participating. Please speak with our club advisor confidentially to discuss options.

Can I join mid-year?

Yes, we accept new members throughout the school year. While joining at the beginning of the year is ideal, we understand that interests develop at different times. Note that competition registration deadlines may limit participation in certain events if you join later in the year.
