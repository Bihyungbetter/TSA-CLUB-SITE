Meet Our Advisor

Get to know the faculty member who guides our TSA chapter.

Ms. Jennifer Reynolds

TSA Faculty Advisor & Technology Education Teacher

Ms. Reynolds has been teaching technology education for over 10 years and has served as the Myers Park TSA advisor since 2015. She holds a Master's degree in Educational Technology and is passionate about helping students develop their technical and leadership skills.

Under her guidance, the Myers Park TSA chapter has won numerous awards at regional and state competitions, and several teams have qualified for the National TSA Conference.
