Our Mission

The Myers Park Technology Student Association (TSA) is dedicated to developing leadership, academic, and business management skills in the context of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Our mission is to inspire and prepare the next generation of technology leaders through hands-on experiences, competitive events, and leadership opportunities. We strive to foster innovation, problem-solving, and collaboration among our members.

As a chapter of the national Technology Student Association, we uphold the values of:

  • Learning and growth through competitive events
  • Leadership development and teamwork
  • Innovation and creative problem-solving
  • Service to our school and community
  • Ethical practices in technology development

Our History

The Myers Park TSA chapter was founded in 2010 by a small group of students passionate about technology and engineering. Under the guidance of our first advisor, Ms. Rebecca Johnson, the club started with just 15 members.

Over the years, our chapter has grown significantly in both size and achievements. We've expanded our focus areas to include robotics, coding, engineering design, digital media, and leadership development.

Key milestones in our history:

2024

Chapter founded with 8 members

2025

First state competition participation with 7 teams

Today, our chapter continues to grow and evolve, adapting to new technologies and challenges while maintaining our commitment to excellence and innovation.
